Pinjun Zheng

About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Program, CEMSE, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), Thuwal, KSA. I am currently working in the Information Science Lab (ISL) with Prof. Tareq Al-Naffouri. Previously, I obtained my B.S. and M.S. degrees both from the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), China. My research interests primarily focus on signal processing and estimation theory, especially in the background of radio localization and communication.

About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Program, CEMSE, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), Thuwal, KSA. I am currently working in the Information Science Lab (ISL) with Prof. Tareq Al-Naffouri. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor and Master degrees both from the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), China. My research interests primarily focus on mmWave/THz localization and communication.

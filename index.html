About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE). I currently work in the Information System Lab (ISL), under the supervision of Prof. Tareq Al-Naffouri. Before that, I got my Bachelor and Master degrees both from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT). My research interests are terahertz localization and phase retrieval problem.
